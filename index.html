The Client

The client is a medical device development and manufacturing organization specializing in minimally invasive device components. The company provides end-to-end support for medical device companies, including design, prototyping, and manufacturing services for catheter-based technologies.

With advanced manufacturing capabilities and specialized engineering expertise, the company helps medical device innovators accelerate development timelines and move products to market more efficiently.

Target Audience

The campaign targeted senior decision-makers within medical device organizations responsible for product development and operational efficiency. This included Chief Operating Officers, Vice Presidents of Manufacturing, R&D Directors, and product development managers.

Many of these professionals work at emerging and mid-size medical device manufacturers that require agile development partners capable of accelerating complex device production timelines.

The Problem

Despite having strong engineering capabilities and an integrated manufacturing model, the company lacked a structured lead generation system. Most new business originated from referrals or existing relationships.

Without an organized outbound marketing process or centralized CRM system, the company struggled to consistently reach new markets and engage potential clients who were unaware of their capabilities.

The Mechanism

Hyper-Targeted Prospect Database
Lucid Strategies developed a highly targeted database of medical device executives and product development leaders within companies actively developing catheter-based and minimally invasive technologies.

Multi-Channel Outreach
The campaign combined LinkedIn messaging, cold email outreach, and direct calls to initiate conversations with qualified prospects across the medical device industry.

Structured Outreach Cadence
Each prospect entered a structured outreach cadence of up to 555 touches across multiple communication channels, ensuring consistent follow-ups and increasing response rates.

Trade Show Reactivation Strategy
Past trade show contacts and dormant leads were reactivated through targeted messaging campaigns, converting previously inactive relationships into new business opportunities.

The Result
Within six months, Lucid Strategies generated 767+ qualified leads, 25+ email information requests, and supported the creation of 22+ deals, producing a lead-to-deal conversion rate of 2.87% while expanding the client’s market reach beyond referral networks.

The outreach campaign successfully introduced the company’s rapid development capabilities to a wider segment of the medical device industry. By positioning speed and integrated manufacturing as a clear market advantage, the campaign generated strong interest among executives seeking faster product development timelines.

The results confirmed that structured outbound engagement can unlock new growth opportunities for highly specialized medical device development firms.
